Cafe Latte

Description

Recipe for creating a hot and enjoyable Cafe Latte!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Hot freshly brewed Dark Roast Espresso coffee

Steps

  1. Heat milk in saucepan over medium-low heat. Whisk briskly with a wire whisk to create foam.
  2. Brew Espresso and pour into cups. Pour in milk, holding back the foam with a spoon. Spoon foam over the top. Enjoy!
