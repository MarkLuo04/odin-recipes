Good Old-Fashioned Pancakes

Description

Recipe for creating easy, delicious pancakes!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • Milk and butter
  • Eggs
  • Maple syrup

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combine.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes, then flip.
  5. Continue cooking until brown on both sides. Pour maple syrup on top of the finished product. Enjoy!
