Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Description

Recipe for creating a delicious high protein Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie!

Ingredients

  • Banana (frozen)
  • Milk
  • Peanut Butter
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Protein Powder - chocolate or vanilla (optional)

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into blender
  2. Blend until smooth. Enjoy!
